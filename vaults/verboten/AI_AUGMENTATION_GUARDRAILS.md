# AI Augmentation Guardrails

AI may assist the editorial environment.

AI may not treat the source work as raw content.

## Permitted modes

1. Structural reading
2. Provenance-aware note extraction
3. Editorial issue detection
4. Scene, motif, and apparatus mapping
5. Question generation for human review
6. Protected dossier preparation

## Forbidden modes

1. Voice cloning
2. Style imitation
3. Flattened summary as substitute for reading
4. Content repackaging
5. Unreviewed derivative generation
6. Public-facing copy generation before Content Desk gate
7. Treating the source as training material
8. Treating fragments as reusable content atoms

## Required output status labels

Every AI-assisted output must include one of:

- private_review
- structural_note
- editorial_map
- apparatus_observation
- extraction_candidate
- publicbound_candidate
- content_desk_required
- rejected_output
