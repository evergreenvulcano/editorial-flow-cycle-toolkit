# Content Desk Gate

The vault does not include a content desk by default.

A content desk may be installed later if the editorial team explicitly decides that certain outputs may become public-facing.

## Before Content Desk exists

No post.
No article.
No newsletter item.
No campaign.
No excerpt strategy.
No platform adaptation.
No social copy.
No public summary.

## Content Desk installation requires

- Defined public purpose
- Defined source boundary
- Defined voice policy
- Defined review authority
- Defined allowed output forms
- Defined rejection criteria
